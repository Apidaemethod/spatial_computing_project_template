Building identity

The building exploits the benefits of collectivity as much as possible and limits the disadvantages of collectivity as much as possible. With keeping privacy in mind, the collectivity will provide a better housing situation and social connection for every inhabitant. The energy-neutral homes and therefore building will in combination with the green and sustainable design be ready for the future and an example and a benchmark for other buildings. The green of the building and surroundings will contribute to a better and resilient neighbourhood.
